Our people

We have specialists in every area of financial planning all of whom are prepared to go the extra mile in delivering unfailing client satisfaction.

Our full-service offering is available to all clients. All our advisers provide completely independent, whole of market, financial advice and we are not tied into any particular products or services.

Most of our team are fluent in English and we also have Spanish, French, Slovak, German and Russian speakers.
Our central European office is in Slovakia and we are further supported through a large network of trusted professional partners and intermediaries worldwide.

Message from the MD

We believe that a well-governed business, ran in a sustainable way, will deliver better overall outcomes for our clients and stronger, more resilient returns, as well as making a positive contribution to our communities and our planet.

The COVID-19 pandemic led to a dramatic loss of human life worldwide and presents an unprecedented challenge to public health, food systems and the world we all work. in We must rethink the future of our environment and tackle environmental degradation with ambition and urgency.

Our six point plan reflects where we are on our journey to becoming sustainable business and we still have much more to do as scientific knowledge, data, regulation, and consumer habits and preferences evolve at lightning pace alongside us.

  • Integrate ESG into our investments and accelerate engagement
  • Growing our planet
  • Setting standards in sustainability and our ESG policies
  • Helping clients understand opportunities, risks and outcomes of sustainable investing
  • Lead collective action to deliver change
  • Aligning people behind our sustainability ambition
