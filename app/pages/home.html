How we work

1

Introduction

A brief meeting to explain our services, discuss your situation and understand and agree on how we may work together to help achieve your financial objectives.

2

Factfinding Meetin

We meet to gather detailed information on your current financial situation, understand in-depth your key goals and objectives, financial priorities, time frames, as well as your attitude to risk.

3

Discovery

Based on the information you provide us with, we analyse your situation and provide you with a detailed financial report with specific proposals.

4

Presentation

Once you have received your financial plan and had time to review it, we sit down to discuss the proposals, answer any questions you may have and agree on the next steps.

5

Action Plan

This is where we put in place the proposals as greed at the previous meeting and start to make your money work for you.

6

Review Meeting

We agree to meet on a regular basis to ensure you remain on track for your financial goals.
