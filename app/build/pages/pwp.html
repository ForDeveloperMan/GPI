Your Personal Wealth Portrait

Welcome to Personal Wealth Portrait (PWP) — a platform that gives you access to view all your finances in one place 24/7 on any web-device. PWP enables you to collate information on your short-term finances like bank account(s), credit cards, loans and mortgages, view your fund information and investment portfolio at the click of a button. So whether you’re looking for an up-to-date valuation of your investments, want to assess how you’re progressing against your goals or simply wish to get in touch, PWP has it covered.
